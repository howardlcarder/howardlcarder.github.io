How It Works

A process built around your workflow

Simple, collaborative, and built on decades of working with busy executives, marketing teams, and editors on deadline.

1

Let's Talk

A short conversation — by phone, email, or whatever works for you — to understand the project, the audience, and what success looks like. No lengthy intake forms.

2

For bylined and case study work, I conduct interviews with your executives, customers, or subject matter experts. I come prepared, ask good questions, and do the homework so you don't have to explain the basics.
